Executive Summary: OTC LATAM consolidates and modernizes our Order-to-Cash systems across Latin America, unifying MIOSS (domestic sales) and SIV (export sales) into a single cloud-native platform. This initiative will serve 500+ users, process 15,000+ orders annually (2024 baseline), and reduce operational costs by $984,000/year (30%) while improving processing speed from 9+ hours to under 4 hours per order (40% faster).
